 External Settings to Low End Devices

So First Go to Settings and Search in the search bar for these settings.
  • Pointer speed=Full
  • Large Mouse Pointer=On
Switch on Developer Options And then make these settings.
  • Windows Animation Scale=Animation Off
  • Transition Animation Scale=Animation Off
  • Animator duration Scale=Animation Off
  • Force 4x MSAA=On
  • Touch and hold delay=search in the settings search bar
  • Touch and Hold delay=Short
  • Auto Click After Pointer Stops=On and Lowest.
